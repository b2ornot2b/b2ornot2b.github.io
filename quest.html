  • Gain Fresh Hero Bonus: Brush teeth and tidy up the bathroom (+10 XP).
  • Master Order: Place dirty clothes in the laundry basket (+10 XP).

  • Complete Morning Feast Mission: Clear the breakfast table (+Rs.10).
  • Unlock Table Champion Badge: Clear the lunch table (+Rs.10).

Daily Missions

  • Prevent Monsters of the Night: Close all windows at dusk (+Rs.10).
  • Earn Gatekeeper Title: Open or close the main gate (+Rs.10).
  • Be a Prepared Prodigy: Pack your school bag for the next day (+Rs.10).

Hygiene and Stamina

  • Unlock Sunny Gear Buff: Take a bath before lunch (*Get-Out-of-Bath-Free Card* can delay this once a month) (+10 XP).
  • Boost Stamina: Dry your towel in the sun after a bath (+10 XP).

Study Mastery

  • Gain Knowledge Points: Spend 1 hour (6:30-7:30 PM) on study, Olympiad prep, or yoga (+10 XP).

Physical Power-Up

  • Level Up Active Hero Skill: Engage in physical activities like badminton or football from 5:00-6:30 PM every day (+20 XP).

Sleep Adventure

  • Unlock Early Bird Title: Be in bed by 10:30 PM (+15 XP).

Helper's Heart

  • Earn Helping Hand Skill: Get Rs.3 for doing chores for others when asked (+Rs.3).

Traps to Avoid

Hygiene and Cleanliness

  • Trigger Toilet Trouble Trap: Leave the toilet seat down before peeing (-Rs.5).
  • Lose Energy to the Electric Vampire: Forget to switch off lights or appliances (-Rs.5).
  • Summon Sockless Goblin: Keep shoes out of the cabinet or without socks (-Rs.5).
  • Fall into the Tooth Decay Pit: Use the phone while brushing teeth (-Rs.5).

Study and Order

  • Invoke Clutter Chaos Curse: Leave the study table messy (-Rs.5).

Food Mistakes

  • Summon Distracted Ogre: Watch videos or use your phone during meals (-Rs.5).
  • Awaken Veggie Monster: Leave food on the plate (-Rs.5).

Respect and Noise

  • Call Forth Rude Spirit: Disrespect elders or make unnecessary noise (-Rs.5).
  • Summon Earache Goblin: Keep the TV volume too high (-Rs.5).

Night-Time Penalties

  • Activate Sleep Depriver Curse: Stay awake past 11:00 PM (-Rs.5).

School Accessories

  • Trigger Lost Treasure Penalty: Lose tiffins, water bottles, or school accessories (-Rs.10).
  • Awaken Forgotten Duty Trap: Fail to remove the tiffin and bottle from the school bag and place them on the dining table before the next day (-Rs.5).

Bonus and Rewards Scheme

  • Unlock Heroic Consistency Reward: Complete all daily missions for two weeks straight (+Rs.50).
  • Gain Top Performer Buff: Earn an extra (+30 XP) for perfect hygiene and study routines.

Monthly Rewards

  • Unlock Golden Star Award: Rack up 300 XP or more in a month and snag (+Rs.200), plus a special treat like a movie night or a favorite dessert! Shine bright, superstar!
  • Level Up Legendary Helper Badge: Be the ultimate sidekick by completing 10 "Helper’s Heart" chores in a month (+Rs.100). You’re a legend in the making!
  • Unlock Champion Streak: Hit bedtime goals (10:30 PM) consistently and bag (+Rs.50). Sleep well, dream big, and win!
  • Earn Perfect Hero Badge: Finish the month penalty-free to unlock the "Perfect Hero" badge and a whopping (+Rs.200)! Be the ultimate quest champion!
